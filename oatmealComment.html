Oatmeal mask

Skin
13 october ,2020

oatmeal is a popular ingredient in food preparation due to its numerous health benefits. But its benefits extend beyond the kitchen. In fact, oatmeal has been used to moisturize and nourish skin for thousands of years. Although there isn’t a lot of research to support the use of oatmeal for skin care, some research does show that it has a moisturizing effect. One of the best ways to take advantage of oatmeal’s moisturizing capabilities is by using it in a skin mask. A skin mask is a conditioning treatment you leave in for an extended period, usually around 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the skin mask ingredients, your skin type, and the degree of conditioning you need. This article will look at the benefits of using an oatmeal skin mask and how to make one and apply it to your skin.

What are the benefits of an oatmeal skin mask?
Little research specifically looks at the way oatmeal may benefit skin, so evidence that supports possible benefits is limited. However, there is research that mineral and vegetable oils can protect skin from damage and breakage and have a moisturizing effect. Although this particular study didn’t focus specifically on oatmeal, the review did state that monounsaturated oils and saturated oils may penetrate the skin shaft more fully than polyunsaturated oils. In other words, using an ingredient like oatmeal on your skin may provide more benefits than a polyunsaturated oil such as sunflower or safflower oil. According to a 2017 review of studiesTrusted Source on the health benefits of oatmeal, a compound called squalene, found in virgin oatmeal, has the ability to act as a moisturizer in various cosmetics. Virgin oatmeal contains vitamins E and other antioxidants. A 2010 studyTrusted Source suggests that a nutrient in the vitamin E family may help promote skin growth..

How to make a oatmeal skin mask?
Instructions:
1- Use one ripe banana and one tablespoon of oatmeal.
2- Blend the banana and then add the oatmeal and continue blending.
3- While in the shower, apply the mask to your skin and scalp with fingers.
4- keep it for 10-15 minutes before rinings
